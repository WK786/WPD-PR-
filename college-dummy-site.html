About Institute

The institute is located virtually in the heart of the country at Nagpur with excellent rail, road & air connectivity, the city boasts of one of the finest urban infrastructures in the entire country.

Mission

To provide quality technical education that fosters innovation, critical thinking, and practical skills, empowering students to excel in their careers and contribute meaningfully to society. We are committed to nurturing a learning environment that promotes professional ethics, holistic development, and lifelong learning.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education, recognized for producing skilled professionals, ethical leaders, and responsible citizens who drive technological advancements and societal progress. We aspire to continuously evolve with industry trends, fostering research, innovation, and global competence among our students.
